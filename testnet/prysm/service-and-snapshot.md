### Live Peers <a href="#live-peers" id="live-peers"></a>

```
PEERS=$(curl -sS https://prysm-rpc.node9x.com/net_info | jq -r '.result.peers[] | "\(.node_info.id)@\(.remote_ip):\(.node_info.listen_addr)"' | awk -F ':' '{print $1":"$(NF)}' | paste -sd, -)
```
